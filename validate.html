Validation Screenshot Helper

How to capture validation screenshots

Follow these steps for each page you implemented:

Step 1: Open the W3C Markup Validation Service at https://validator.w3.org/#validate_by_upload
Step 2: Click "Choose File" and select the HTML file from your local drive
Step 3: Click "Check" to validate
Step 4: Take a screenshot of the results (use Win + Shift + S on Windows)
Step 5: Save the screenshot to validationSnapshots/ with the filename shown below
