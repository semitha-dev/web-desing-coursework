Reflection on Validation

The feedback form initially had a validation error because I used a <label> element without a matching for attribute on one field. Adding the correct for attribute resolved the issue. I also discovered that the maxlength attribute on a textarea must be a non-negative integer, which I had initially left empty. Setting it to 500 fixed the error.

This process taught me to validate incrementally as I add form elements rather than waiting until the entire form is complete.
